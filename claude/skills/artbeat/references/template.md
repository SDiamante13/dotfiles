## Notes on tone

- **Client-facing sections**: Professional but not stiff. Show momentum and competence. Use specific outcomes ("deployed through Stage", "confirmed X doesn't support Y, proposing alternative") not vague activity ("worked on", "continued").
- **Internal sections**: Direct and honest. This is where you flag soft concerns that aren't blockers yet but could become ones.
- **Bullet points**: Start with the bolded topic/feature name, then the status or outcome. Include ticket numbers when known.
